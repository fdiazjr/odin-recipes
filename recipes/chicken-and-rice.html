Chicken and Rice

Ingredients

  • Chicken
  • Jasmin or Bismati Rice
  • Bone Broth

Steps

  1. Bring oven to 350 degree fahrenheit
  2. Season chicken and cook in oven for 20 min and then flip and cook another 30 min
  3. Add chicken and bone broth to rice cooker
  4. Place in tupperware
